# **App Name**: Glam House Digital

## Core Features:

- Multi-Page Navigation: Seamless navigation between Home, Services, Academy, Gallery, About, Reviews, Contact, Booking, and Enrollment pages with responsive menu and active link highlighting.
- Dynamic Theming: User-toggleable dark and light modes, persisting across sessions and smoothly transitioning elements.
- Functional Booking Form: An interactive form for booking salon services, capturing customer details, service, date, and time preferences, with local storage persistence for form data and direct WhatsApp redirection for confirmation.
- Course Enrollment Form: A comprehensive form for academy course enrollment, collecting student information, course interest, and educational background, with pre-filled fields based on navigation and direct WhatsApp redirection for inquiries.
- Interactive Content Display: Visually appealing presentation of services, courses, and galleries using structured layouts, carousels, and filterable grids.
- Mobile Responsiveness: Ensures a fluid and optimal user experience across all devices and screen sizes, from mobile to desktop.
- Direct WhatsApp Communication: Integration of direct WhatsApp links for easy booking confirmations, enrollment inquiries, and general contact, with pre-filled messages to streamline user interaction.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light Mode Primary Accent: A vibrant, elegant pink (#E91E8C) used for calls-to-action and key interactive elements, reflecting the brand's 'Glam' identity.
- Light Mode Background: A clean, luminous white (#FFFFFF) with a soft blush pink secondary background (#FFF5F8) for subtle depth and warmth, creating an inviting canvas.
- Light Mode Accent Gold: A rich, warm gold (#C9A84C) to signify premium quality and sophistication, used for complementary accents and luxurious details.
- Dark Mode Primary Accent: A brilliant, deep fuchsia pink (#FF2D9B) for high contrast and strong emphasis against the dark background, maintaining the brand's vibrant energy.
- Dark Mode Background: A deep, opulent charcoal (#0D0D0D) with an intense reddish-black secondary background (#1A0A10), providing a dramatic and modern aesthetic.
- Dark Mode Accent Gold: A brighter, antique gold (#D4A853) that stands out on dark backgrounds, enhancing the premium feel in the evening theme.
- Display and Headings: 'Playfair Display' (serif), chosen for its elegant and artistic high-contrast letterforms, suitable for conveying a luxurious and editorial feel.
- Body Text: 'DM Sans' (sans-serif), providing excellent legibility and a modern, professional appearance for longer content sections and forms.
- Accent Script: 'Dancing Script' (script), utilized for taglines and delicate cursive elements, adding a touch of personalized charm and elegance. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Clean and modern icons from Lucide React, featuring essential UI elements like sun/moon for theme toggle, phone, social media, and descriptive icons for services and trust badges, all in brand colors.
- Full mobile responsiveness across all pages, dynamic grid systems for service/course cards and galleries, a sticky navigation bar with a mobile-first hamburger menu, and specific hero section layouts with image placeholders and text overlays.
- Subtle and engaging animations powered by Framer Motion, including staggered fade-ins on page load, scroll-triggered reveals for sections, gentle lift-and-shadow effects on card hovers, shimmer/glow for CTA buttons, and smooth auto-scrolling carousels.
